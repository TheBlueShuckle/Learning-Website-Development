Entry Area: Part 1

Image of the first part of the Entry Area of The Witness

This is the first area you will encounter in the game. It contains 6 Puzzle panels that you will have to solve in order to access the rest of the island.
In order to open the locked gate, you will have to follow the cables on the ground which leads to three panels scattered around the castle. Whenever
you solve one these, a new section of the puzzle unlocking the gate will open.
